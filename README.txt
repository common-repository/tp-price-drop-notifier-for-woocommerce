TP Price Drop Notifier for WooCommerce PRO is a very powerful extension for your woocommerce store,
Give your customers a new option to track product price and track products that are back to stock.

== Description ==

* Let your customers know when the price of the product has dropped.
* Track of your customer's most wanted products.
* Learn what products , customers want it's price to be dropped.
* Add "product back to stock" track notifier.
* 100% Responsive and mobile friendly.

TP Price Drop Notifier for WooCommerce PRO add to your product's page new form with several fields:
1. Name
2. Email
3. Price lower than (optional), (Maximal price your customer is willing to pay)
4. Add Checkbox for Newsletter (optional)

After customer subscribe, his request is saved in your store and waiting for a price drop.
There is a new cron job that run every 3 houres and check if the price of the product has dropped / back to stock, If so, the customers will receive an e-mail about it.

In your admin area you can see all customers and their requests (Who received an e-mail, Who is waiting for email), statistics and much more‚Ä¶

3. Activate TP Price Drop Notifier for WooCommerce through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
